Leadership

Vitanova Organization is led by Murtaza, a dynamic leader committed to transforming lives through education and empowerment. The organization brings together a diverse team of professionals, including educators, scholarship advisors, and industry experts, alongside enthusiastic students and volunteers. With a strong focus on providing free courses, scholarship guidance, and skill development opportunities, Vitanova aims to bridge the gap in education access. The team is driven by a shared vision to create impactful learning experiences, offer valuable certificates, and foster personal and professional growth.
